About the Band

Glocktopus... A fantastic mixture of all things good. At it's core, a combination of two words: Glock, and topus. Glock comes from the streets, and topus is probably latin for fucking awesome.

All of the members of Glocktopus are too old to be playing this kind of music and none of their friends think it's cool anymore, yet they just wont stop. Is it commitment? Probably mental illness. No-one tell them that.

The music is non-phalangerous. The way they paly music will make you think: "Do they even have phalanges?"
Yes. They do. Science has not yet come far enough to remove them, so for now they suffer..

In summary, Glocktopus are great. Even better than a $20 sandwich from a street vendor that is still looking for his fathers approval. Come see them. Join them. Be them.
